    • About GivingWay

    GivingWay is an online social network that directly connects NGOs and volunteers from all around the world. GivingWay makes volenteering easy, accessible, and affordable while leaving out the middlemen and agent fees.

    How We Work

    1. Choose whether you want to volunteer online or while traveling abroad and then descide on what region interests you.

    2. Check out their causes and activities and what types of volunteers and support they need.

    3. Hit the “Apply” button to start the application process with nonprofits that interest you.

    4. Once your application has been approved by the nonprofit it’s time to get moving!
